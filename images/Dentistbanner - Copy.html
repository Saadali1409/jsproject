Your dentist in Enschede Stokhorst

Dental Clinics Enschede Stokhorst & Breuch is the dentist for you and your family in Enschede Stokhorst and the surrounding area. You can find our practice at Chrysantstraat 6 in Enschede. Our experienced dentists, dental hygienists and dental assistants are happy to help you make and keep your teeth as healthy as possible. Please find the English translation here.

What can you expect from us?

  • We are an experienced team of dentists, dental hygienists and dental assistants.
  • We work with state-of-the-art equipment.
  • We are open 6 days a week, including Saturdays and evenings!
  • In addition to Dutch, we speak the following languages: English, German, French, Hungarian, Polish, Czech and Turkish.
  • You can park your car for free at the practice.
  • We offer a wide range of treatments: from teeth whitening with ZOOM (Philips) and the placement of invisible braces from Invisalign and Clear Correct to treating inflamed gums and placing implants.

Looking for a dental hygienist in Enschede?

Experienced dental hygienists work in our dental office. They specialize in getting and keeping your teeth healthy. Oral hygienists Kbra and Viktria have extra attention for anxious patients.

Are you looking for a good dental hygienist because, for example, you suffer from sensitive teeth, bleeding or receding gums, bad breath or your teeth are a little loose? Then make an appointment right away by calling 0537 900 000.

Taking your child to the dentist: free oral care up to age 18

Healthy teeth give children a lifetime of pleasure. But you have to do something for them. This applies to children from the moment their first teeth start to erupt. From this time children can meet the dentist free of charge.

Did you know that the basic insurance covers dental checkups and care until your child's 18th year (with the exception of orthodontics, among others)? So children up to age 18 can go to the dentist for free! That way, nothing stands in the way of laying a healthy foundation for both baby teeth and permanent teeth.

Implantology and endodontology

In our dental practice, we offer a wide range of treatments. For example, you can come to us for root canal treatments (endodontology) and the placement of implants (implantology).

When the nerve of a tooth is damaged, it can lead to inflammation in the mouth. In such a case, it is necessary to perform root canal treatment.

An implant is placed to replace one, several or even all of your teeth. This allows you to laugh, eat and talk like you used to.

Fear of the dentist? We take that into account!

Did you know that as many as 40% of Dutch adults report being afraid of the dentist? Fear of pain is the most common reason. But fear can also come from previous experiences, stories from others or embarrassment about teeth. Wherever your fear comes from, we take it very seriously. For example, we schedule extra time to explain the treatment to you step by step. Do you not feel the need for this? Let your therapist know prior to the treatment. This way we can make the treatment as comfortable as possible, tailored to your needs.

Invisible braces with Invisalign and Clear Correct

We offer aligner treatments. Aligners are virtually invisible covers that are placed over teeth. This straightens teeth in an almost invisible way. Learn more about aligners on this page.

For more complex dental abnormalities, we refer you to an orthodontist. An orthodontist specializes in correcting (larger) dental defects, such as crooked teeth, an overbite or underbite. Are you looking for a good orthodontist? Then we would like to refer you to TopOrtho Hengelo.

Canceling your appointment

To cancel your appointment, we ask that you cancel by phone or email no later than 24 hours in advance. This way we may be able to help another patient and do not have to charge you.
